// WHY IT MATTERS

Don't wait until it's too late

Data loss happens in ways you can't always predict. The right backup strategy protects you from all of them.

Ransomware & Malware

If your files are encrypted by an attacker, a clean backup means you can recover without paying a ransom.

Hardware Failure

Hard drives, phones, and servers fail without warning. Backups ensure a failure doesn't mean permanent data loss.

Accidental Deletion

Human error is one of the most common causes of data loss. Versioned backups let you roll back to any previous point.

Natural Disasters

Fire, flood, or theft can wipe out local storage instantly. Offsite cloud backups keep your data safe no matter what.
